HOCKEY

Hockey is a game that is played around the world by many countries. Some of the top hockey playing nations includes India, Pakistan, New Zealand, Netherlands and Great Britain.

It is playing in different types of both summer and winter team sports which is an either out-door-field, or dry floor or sheet of ice.

The object of hockey is to hit a ball with a stick into a goal. Every time the ball goes into the goal then a point is awarded to that team. The team with the most goals at the end of the game is awarded the winner. In the event of both teams scoring the same amount of goals then a draw is called.

There are many types of hockey.Such as, ice hockey, roller hockey, rink hockey, field hockey, and floor hockey.

Some are neumerous types that they make use of skates eother bladded or wheeled while others do not.In each of these sports, two brigades play each other other by trying to put the disk or ball into the opponent's thing using a hockey stick.

Some basic rules of hockey are Legal teams are five skaters and goalie, Players may play on only one team, Games are thirty minutes straight play, No over-time during the regular season, No slap shots at all - not during warm-ups or games.

Some equipments used for hockey are a helmet with face mask, shin pads and gloves, skates(for ice hockey), goalie sticks.

Field hockey, also called hockey, outdoor game played by two opposing teams of 11 players each who use sticks curved at the striking end to hit a small, hard ball into their opponent’s goal. It is called field hockey to distinguish it from the similar game played on ice.
